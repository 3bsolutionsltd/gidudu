Stories of Impact

Real lives transformed by the love and support of our sponsors and donors

A few of our children whose lives have been transformed by the love and support of our sponsors and donors around the world. We couldn't be more proud.

Allan Wandera's Story

From a life of struggle and uncertainty, Allan found hope and purpose through Berakhah Childcare. Today, he is excelling in his studies and dreams of becoming a doctor to serve his community. His transformation is a testament to the power of love, education, and faith working together.

"I never thought I would go to school. Now I dream of helping others like people helped me."
Sponsor Allan

Kabuya Prisca's Story

Prisca suffered a serious eye injury that threatened her education and future. Through the Berakhah program, she received not only medical care but also a loving family that nurtured her gifts. Today, she is thriving in school and dreams of becoming a doctor to help others like her.

"IGFM gave me hope when I thought I would never see clearly again. They showed me God's love is real."
Sponsor Prisca

Wanadwadi Joseph's Story

Joseph lost his father at a young age and his early years were marked by poverty and uncertainty. Berakhah Childcare welcomed him with open arms, providing him with proper nutrition, medical care, and a quality education. Now a confident teenager, Joseph is thriving academically and showing natural leadership abilities.

"I want to give back to Berakhah what they gave to me - hope and a future."
Sponsor Joseph

Muwanguzi Eria's Story

Eria came to Berakhah after losing his father, unable to attend school due to extreme poverty. Through comprehensive care including healthcare, education, and spiritual mentorship, Eria has been completely transformed. He is now excelling in his studies, loves football and singing, and actively participates in church activities.

"God used Berakhah to save my life. Now I want to serve Him and help others."
Sponsor Eria
