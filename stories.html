Stories of Impact

Real lives transformed by the love and support of our sponsors and donors

A few of our children whose lives have been transformed by the love and support of our sponsors and donors around the world. We couldn't be more proud.

Allan Wandera's Story

From a life of struggle and uncertainty, Allan found hope and purpose through Berakhah Childcare. Today, he is excelling in his studies and dreams of becoming a doctor to serve his community. His transformation is a testament to the power of love, education, and faith working together.

"I never thought I would go to school. Now I dream of helping others like people helped me."

Moreen's Story

Moreen lost both her parents at a young age and faced an uncertain future. Through the Berakhah program, she received not only education and healthcare but also a loving family that nurtured her gifts. Today, she is part of the Berakhah Children's Choir, using her voice to inspire others and share hope.

"IGFM gave me a family when I had none. They showed me God's love is real."

Loyce's Story

Abandoned as a baby, Loyce's early years were marked by poverty and neglect. Berakhah Childcare welcomed her with open arms, providing her with proper nutrition, medical care, and a quality education. Now a confident teenager, Loyce is thriving academically and mentoring younger children in the program.

"I want to give back to Berakhah what they gave to me - hope and a future."

Gerald's Story

Gerald came to Berakhah malnourished and unable to attend school due to extreme poverty. Through comprehensive care including healthcare, education, and spiritual mentorship, Gerald has been completely transformed. He is now one of the top students in his class and actively participates in church activities, sharing his testimony with others.

"God used Berakhah to save my life. Now I want to serve Him and help others."
