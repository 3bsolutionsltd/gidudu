Who We Are

International Great Faith Ministries

International Great Faith Ministries (IGFM) is a non-denominational ministry bringing hope to unreached communities in Uganda and beyond.

We share God's love through church planting, evangelism, leadership training, and caring for the vulnerable via Berakhah Childcare. We also serve rural villages by meeting educational, physical, and healthcare needs.

Spreading Hope Through Music

Berakhah Children's Choir

The Berakhah Children's Choir shares their life-changing testimonies, hope, and joy through vibrant cultural performances and extraordinary talent.

As they travel and perform, these remarkable children use Uganda's rich heritage to raise awareness about the challenges faced by orphans and vulnerable children in rural communities.
