Prisca's Story

Prisca is 14 years old and has endured tremendous hardship throughout her childhood. She had her left eye damaged by a neighbor's child and couldn't see well, a similar condition which occurred to her father at the same age of 9 which has failed him from active work up-to-date. This led Prisca to occasionally have severe headaches, poor vision, pain, and deterioration on her performance.

However this problem was handled medically when she joined Berakhah - her sight has been fully restored. With the father's failing sight and her mother's failing health, these have affected Prisca's parents in getting an income to support the family. Prisca would help around the house by washing plates, sweeping and cleaning the compound, but the basic needs of the family were not met adequately.

Prisca is in primary seven and dreams of becoming a Doctor. At Berakhah childcare, she has been welcomed with love and acceptance into her new family and has great comfort that her basic needs like food, shelter and education are being met.
