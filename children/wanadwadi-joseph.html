Joseph's Story

Joseph is a kind and compassionate 15-year-old who has overcome significant hardships. He lost his father at a young age, leaving his mother to care for five children alone. Life was extremely difficult, and Joseph often felt hopeless about his future. Education seemed like an impossible dream.

When Berakhah welcomed Joseph, he found not just a place to live, but a family that believed in him. Joseph is now thriving in secondary school, where he excels in his studies and shows natural leadership abilities. He loves helping others and dreams of becoming a social worker to help vulnerable children like himself.

Joseph is grateful for the opportunity Berakhah has given him and works hard every day to make the most of it.
