Who We Are

International Great Faith Ministries

International Great Faith Ministries (IGFM) is a non-denominational ministry bringing hope to communities in Uganda, Kenya and the USA.

We share God's love through church planting, evangelism, leadership training, and caring for vulnerable via Berakhah Childcare. We also serve rural villages by meeting educational, physical, and healthcare needs.

IGFM is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit in the USA and a registered nonprofit in Uganda.

Our Leadership

About Our Founder

Pastor Paul Gidudu

A dynamic leader, visionary, and passionate servant of God

Pastor Paul Gidudu has been in full-time ministry for 30 years. He is a dynamic leader, visionary, and passionate servant of God from Mbale, Eastern Uganda. He is the founder and senior pastor of International Great Faith Ministries, leading a network of over 1,000 rural pastors and taking the Gospel to some of the most remote villages in Uganda.

Through Berakhah Childcare, he ministers to more than 5,000 children and 700 widows, providing education, medical care, and the much-needed love of Christ.

As Senior Pastor of Call2Prayer Church, Pastor Paul mentors emerging leaders, oversees multiple churches, and serves as a spiritual father to many. He also ministers internationally, sharing a powerful testimony that inspires hope and transforms lives.

A devoted husband to Eve and father to their children, he is committed to seeing lives changed and impacted for God's glory.
