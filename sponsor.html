Why Sponsor a Child?

Child sponsorship is one of the most effective ways to help vulnerable children. Your monthly support provides a child with education, healthcare, nutritious meals, spiritual care, and hope for a brighter future.

Education

School fees, uniforms, books, and supplies

Healthcare

Medical care, check-ups, and medications

Nutrition

Three nutritious meals daily

Spiritual Care

Biblical teaching and mentorship
